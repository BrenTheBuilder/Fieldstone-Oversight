The Reality

Contractors are incentivized to maximize their profit. Not protect yours.

Overbilling, inflated schedules, and vague change orders are standard tactics. Without a trained eye on your project, you won't catch it until the money is gone. Fieldstone is that trained eye.

What Owners Face

The problems we solve.

01

Overbilling on Pay Applications

Contractors routinely bill ahead of work completed. Without someone who knows what to look for, you approve invoices for work that isn't done and materials that aren't on site.

02

Schedule Manipulation

Inflated schedules, phantom milestones, and delay claims are standard tactics. An independent schedule review exposes them before they become costly disputes.

03

Change Order Abuse

Poorly scoped contracts give contractors leverage to issue change orders for work that should have been included. We catch these before you sign.

04

Quality You Cannot Verify

Without construction knowledge, you cannot assess workmanship, material compliance, or code adherence. We can. And we document everything.
