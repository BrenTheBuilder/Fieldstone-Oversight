Full Oversight Services

Ongoing & Project-Based Engagements

For projects that need consistent oversight from start to finish, we offer owner representation and project-level consulting priced based on scope and project size.

01

Owner Representation

We act as your construction expert from pre-construction through closeout. You get the benefit of a professional owner's rep without the cost of a full in-house team.

  • Contract review and negotiation support
  • Regular site visits and progress reporting
  • Pay application review each billing cycle
  • Schedule monitoring and delay analysis
  • Contractor communication and issue resolution
  • Owner reporting throughout the project

02

Schedule Review & Analysis

An independent look at your contractor's project schedule to identify unrealistic timelines, potential delay claims, and critical path risks before they become problems.

  • Critical path method (CPM) schedule review
  • Delay and impact analysis
  • Schedule realism assessment
  • Written findings and recommendations

03

Pre-Construction Consulting

Get expert eyes on your project before construction begins. We help you understand what you're buying, who you're hiring, and what could go wrong.

  • Contractor bid evaluation and leveling
  • Budget and scope gap review
  • Contract language review
  • Risk identification memo
  • Pre-construction meeting support

04

Construction Progress Reports

Professional site visit reports that document the condition and progress of your project. Useful for lenders, investors, partners, or your own records.

  • Structured site visit documentation
  • Organized photo sets with annotations
  • Issue and punch list tracking
  • Safety observation notes
  • Report delivered within 24 hours of visit

Owner Representation is priced per project.

Every project is different. Scope, budget, complexity, and duration all affect what level of oversight makes sense. We'll talk through your project and give you a straightforward quote with no retainer packages or hourly guesswork.
