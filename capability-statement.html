Fieldstone Oversight provides independent construction oversight and owner representation for property owners, developers, and investors who need a professional construction expert in their corner — without the cost of a full in-house team. We protect owners from contractor overbilling, poor workmanship, and schedule manipulation on projects up to $20M.

Background

Fieldstone Oversight was founded by Brennan O'Quinn, a commercial construction superintendent with 10+ years of field experience on projects ranging from $10M to $150M across school, healthcare, and commercial sectors in Delaware and the surrounding region.

That experience — managing subcontractors, reviewing pay applications, tracking schedules, and catching the problems that office-based consultants miss — is now available directly to smaller owners and investors who typically can't access this level of expertise.
